G-SCAN PLUS

Privacy Policy  ·  Effective Date: April 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to G-Scan Plus, a guard patrol management application designed for security businesses and enterprise clients. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use the G-Scan Plus mobile application and related services.

By using G-Scan Plus, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the application.

2. Who We Are

G-Scan Plus is operated as a B2B (business-to-business) service. Access to the application is granted exclusively to verified organizations and their authorized personnel. Individual end users access the application only through their employer or contracting organization.

3. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to deliver the patrol management service.

3.1 Account Information

When an organization sets up user accounts, we collect:

  • Full name of authorized personnel
  • Employee or officer ID assigned by the organization
  • Login credentials (passwords are stored in encrypted form and never stored in plain text)

3.2 Location and GPS Data

G-Scan Plus collects precise location (GPS) data from mobile devices during active patrol shifts. This data is used to:

  • Record checkpoint scan events at designated patrol points
  • Verify that guards are present at required locations
  • Generate patrol route reports for supervisors and clients

Location data is only collected while the application is actively in use during a shift. We do not collect location data in the background when the app is closed or when no shift is active.

3.3 Patrol Activity Data

We record scan events, shift start and end times, checkpoint visit logs, and any incident reports submitted through the app. This data is tied to the user account and the assigned shift.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate users and manage access to the platform
  • To record and verify patrol checkpoint scans
  • To generate shift reports and patrol audit trails for client organizations
  • To support offline-first functionality — data is synced to our servers when the device reconnects
  • To investigate and resolve technical issues or disputes
  • To improve the reliability and performance of the application

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the delivery of the G-Scan Plus service.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade any user data to third parties. Data may be shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With your employer or contracting organization: Patrol and location data is visible to authorized supervisors and administrators within your organization as part of the service.
  • Legal compliance: We may disclose information if required to do so by law, court order, or regulatory authority.
  • Business continuity: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or transfer of assets, user data may be transferred to the successor entity, subject to equivalent privacy protections.

6. Data Retention

Patrol and location data is retained for as long as required by the contracting organization or as necessary to fulfill legal obligations. Organizations may request deletion of their data by contacting us directly.

Upon termination of an organization's contract, all associated data will be deleted or anonymized within 90 days, unless a longer retention period is required by applicable law.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include:

  • Encrypted data transmission using HTTPS/TLS
  • Encrypted storage of authentication credentials
  • Role-based access controls within the platform
  • Offline-first data handling with secure local storage on device

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. User Rights

As a user of G-Scan Plus, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data held about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to your organization's data retention obligations)
  • Withdraw consent to data processing, where applicable

To exercise these rights, contact your organization's G-Scan Plus administrator or reach out to us directly using the contact details below.

9. Children's Privacy

G-Scan Plus is intended exclusively for use by adults in a professional capacity. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under the age of 18. If we become aware that a minor has provided data through the platform, we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify users through the application or by other appropriate means. The updated policy will indicate the new effective date at the top of this document.

Continued use of the application after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
